Couch 2 Court — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Couch 2 Court does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no tracking, and it makes no network requests. There are no ads and no third-party SDKs that collect data.

Data stored on your device

Your progress (current level, session history, and settings) is stored only on your device, using local storage. It never leaves your phone. Deleting the app removes this data.

Permissions

The app plays spoken cues and beeps and can show a Live Activity timer on the lock screen and Dynamic Island during a session. It does not request access to your location, contacts, microphone, camera, or health data.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
