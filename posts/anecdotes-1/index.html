Bitcoin, Failing and Moving out - Anecdotes

I’ve been an advocate of moving out as an instrument of growing for a few years now. Of course, I come from some place of privilege: Two loving parents and financial, mental and bodily capability. Please weigh your own situation before moving out.

I first moved out from North India to South India to study Computer Science. The University I attended is an authoritative hellhole. That did not sit well with me, and I mentally checked out from the Uni, focusing on building things, studying on my own and having my own experiences. As a result, I failed in about 15 subjects. This was a bad thing, of course, but it set me free. When I achieved as much as my peers with perfect grades, it gave me confidence. My situation was salvaged by a love for Computer Science.

Indian Academic System is built on a system of oppression of free thoughts, personality and creativity. I present to you this: Asking your student to dress uniformly is a method of oppression.
